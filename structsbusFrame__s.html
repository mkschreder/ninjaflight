uint8_t sbusFrame_s::endByte

The endByte is 0x00 on FrSky and some futaba RX's, on Some SBUS2 RX's the value indicates the telemetry byte that is sent after every 4th sbus frame.

See https://github.com/ninjaflight/ninjaflight/issues/590#issuecomment-101027349 and https://github.com/ninjaflight/ninjaflight/issues/590#issuecomment-101706023
